Dreamy Denz Rentals

At Dreamy Denz, we love bringing your vision to life—and that includes combining our magical setups and adding beautiful balloon enhancements! You're welcome to mix and match any of our soft play rentals and bounce houses for a discounted rate based on your custom package. Want to add that extra pop? We offer balloon add-ons for any setup, including garlands on bounce houses or styled backdrops perfect for photos. Balloon pricing is custom and will be quoted after discussing the size and style you have in mind. Let's create a celebration that's as unique and unforgettable as your event!
